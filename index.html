So what's this all about?

We're committed to identifying and investing in the most environmentally responsible and cost-effective ways to provide reliable and increasingly clean energy to meet customers' demand now and in the future. CEC is driving the emerging technology and innovations needed for a carbon-free future.

We believe that a big part of this mission is to ensure our customers have access to the most efficient and sustainable energy solutions available today. The decisions we make today will determine our long-term goals, and we're committed to making the right choices for each of our customers to have the greatest experience today, tomorrow, and for years to come.
